# LeadFlow AI - Agent Guide
No tech skills required. Just plug it in and let it work.

## What Is This?
LeadFlow AI runs on your Mac Mini 24/7. Every few hours it searches for homeowners ready to sell and writes personalized outreach for each one. You just send the message.

## What You Need
- Mac Mini (M1 or newer) ~$599
- Claude API Key (console.anthropic.com) ~$5-15/month
- Internet connection

## Step 1 - Install
1. Open the folder called realestate-lead-engine
2. Double-click install.sh
3. If Mac asks "Are you sure?" click Open
4. Wait 10-15 minutes for completion
5. Done!

## Step 2 - First Launch
1. Double-click "Start LeadFlow" on your Desktop
2. Fill in the setup screen:
   - Your Name
   - City A (e.g. San Jose, CA)
   - City B (e.g. San Francisco, CA)
   - Radius (15 miles is a good start)
   - Claude API Key (starts with sk-ant-)
3. Click Save & Launch

## Step 3 - Find Leads
Click the big "Scan for Leads" button. Wait 30 seconds.
The AI will score every lead 1-10 and write outreach for hot leads.

## Understanding Lead Colors
- RED (Hot, score 7-10): Contact TODAY
- ORANGE (Warm, score 4-6): Follow up this week  
- BLUE (Cold, score 1-3): Add to nurture list

## Sending Outreach
- Click "Copy Email" -> paste into Gmail -> send
- Click "Copy SMS" -> paste into your texting app -> send
- Click lead card -> read the Voicemail Script

## FAQ
Q: Does this replace my CRM?
A: No - export leads as CSV and import into your CRM.

Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes. Everything stays on your Mac Mini.

Q: How much does Claude cost?
A: $5-15/month. Only hot leads use Claude.

Q: Any issues?
A: Dashboard won't open? Double-click Start LeadFlow again.
   No leads? Check your city names in Settings (gear icon).
